Cornwall Tech Week

about cornwall tech week

Cornwall has an amazing tech and digital sector, but you might not know it. There are many businesses operating on a global scale working with some of the big names, but it's not always obvious.

We want to change that during Tech Week. We hope to raise the aspirations of the next generation by letting them know of the great digital companies on their doorstep, we want to help teachers delivering the new national computing curriculum and we want to have fun!
