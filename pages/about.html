About

A week of events and activities that celebrate and showcase the Cornish Tech industries, raising awareness, aspirations and opportunties.

Who We are

Software Cornwall and The Cornwall College Group have partnered together to raise the profile of the tech sector in Cornwall. We want to show the people of Cornwall and beyond all the great companies and groups that make up the tech sector on their doorstep.

The week will engage children and adults, primary and secondary schools, libraries and employers with the aim of raising aspirations, increasing the visibility of tech and digital careers and putting Cornwall on the national and international map as a great place to live, work and develop in the tech industry.

The Plan

The week will coincide with the word wide event An Hour of Code which will provide a platform for engaging the public. Employers, schools, libraries and other venues will be encouraged to open their doors to the public and run an hour of code event. For employers we will encourage them to host school visits to highlight tech careers.
Over the coming weeks we will publish guides and templates for sessions and promotional material to make running events.

Get Involved

To make this a success we need the Cornish Tech Industry, Education and Public Services to join in and use the opportunity to showcase what happens in the county. Please get in touch through the contact us page and let us know your plans.
