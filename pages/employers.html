Employers/businesses

The purpose of the week is to celebrate the Cornish Tech Industry. We want to raise the aspirations of our children and show them the opportunities for great careers on their doorstep in our amazing county. Oh, and have fun!

If are you part of the Cornish Tech Community you could:

Have an open day
Run a headline event.

Has your workplace got that Wow factor that will inspire and enthuse visitors? Could you dedicate a day to open your doors and with support from our team of volunteers invite one or two schools in for an hour of code session.

Have an organised tour of your workplace

meet the team and explain what everybody does, if you have the equipment, register on hourofcode.org and let your visitors do an activity from code.org. Get in touch and we’ll help with the next steps.

Link up with your local primary or secondary school

Help them run an hour of code for their students and student parents.

Support the week by co-hosting an event

If your work place isn’t suitable for bringing vistors in, could you and your team us organise some headlines events such as the launch event as Eden. We’ll obviously advertise your company and you could still do a small pitch to explain your business.

Promote yourself on our website

We’d love to list all the tech businesses in the county, plus Software Cornwall are building a map of the Cornish Software industry global impact, we would like to include your global customers locations.
